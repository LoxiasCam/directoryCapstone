How It Works

St. Isatis can help you find your next course, in just four simple steps!

1-Search

Being able to search by keywords like name, city or subject, can help you find a better course. You also have multiple categories and libraries at your disposal, so that you can better refine your results.

2-Locate

Once you have found your course of choice, you'll be able to check more information about the course by clicking on it. Is it an in-person course only or do they also have online options? You'll also be able to locate it on the map so you can learn more about its whereabouts!

3-Apply

Apply to your dream course! Depending on your choice and the requirements of the course, you might need to go through an interview, in which case you can use the website's map to locate the course location. You can also check the status of your application status on the website.

4-Learn

After signing up for the course, it is now time to learn! You can check our blog for recommendations so that you can succeed at your course.
